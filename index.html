Meet the Town

Mountainwood, New England

Elsie Harper

Innkeeper of the Mountainwood Inn, and the town's most reluctant amateur detective. Sharp-eyed, stubborn, and always the last one to leave a mystery unsolved.

Murphy

Border collie, professional troublemaker, occasional hero. Murphy has a nose for trouble long before Elsie does — and no patience for staying out of it.

Dr. Tom Dawson

Local physician, Elsie's steady partner, and the voice of reason she doesn't always listen to. Usually right. Rarely thanked for it.
