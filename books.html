The Series

The Mountainwood Mysteries

Eight published mysteries—and another coming soon—featuring innkeeper Elsie Harper, Dr. Tom Dawson, and Murphy, a border collie with excellent instincts. Read them in order, or begin wherever a mystery catches your eye.

Book 1

Murder in Cloverdale

The mystery that begins the series and introduces readers to Elsie Harper and the secrets surrounding Mountainwood.

Book 2

Murder at the Mountainwood Inn

A full house of guests, a storm, and a killer who may never have left the property turn Elsie's inn into a crime scene.

Book 3

Secret at the Stables

The Mountainwood stables hide more than horses when Elsie and Murphy uncover a mystery tied to long-buried secrets.

Book 4

Murder in the Rough

A murder on the golf course reveals that polished country-club manners can conceal dangerous motives.

Book 5

Murder at the Yacht Club

A peaceful day by the water becomes a deadly investigation when trouble surfaces at the yacht club.

Book 6

The Town Council Killing

Small-town politics turn deadly, forcing Elsie to untangle influence, corruption, and murder.

Book 7

A Murder Too Far

A dead woman, a missing laptop, and a small mountain town with more secrets than ski runs.

Book 8

Cold Comfort

A winter mystery on the mountain leaves Elsie facing danger, silence, and questions no one wants answered.
