The Series

The Mountainwood Mysteries

Eight books (and counting) of small-town murder, solved by an innkeeper, her doctor boyfriend, and a border collie with excellent instincts. Read in order, or jump in wherever looks good.

Book One Murder in Cloverdale Sarah Lundin

Book 1

The book that starts it all — Elsie Harper trades city life for a run-down New England inn, and finds her first body before she's even finished unpacking.

Book Two Murder at the Mountainwood Inn Sarah Lundin

Book 2

A full house of guests, one very inconvenient storm, and a killer who never left the property. Elsie's inn becomes the scene of the crime.

Book Three Secret at the Stables Sarah Lundin

Book 3

Mountainwood's riding stables hide more than horses. Murphy sniffs out trouble before Elsie can, in a mystery that digs into the town's oldest families.

Book Four Murder in the Rough Sarah Lundin

Book 4

A body on the ninth hole turns the town's golf club upside down, and Elsie discovers that country club manners hide country club secrets.

Book Five Murder at the Yacht Club Sarah Lundin

Book 5

Summer on the water turns deadly when a fixture of the Mountainwood Yacht Club is found overboard — and everyone on the dock had a reason to push.

Book Six The Town Council Killing Sarah Lundin

Book 6

A contentious town council vote ends in murder, and Elsie learns that small-town politics can be more dangerous than anyone let on.

Book Seven Cold Comfort Sarah Lundin

Book 7

A weekend at the ski lodge turns into a whiteout murder investigation. Snowed in with a killer, Elsie has nowhere to run and nothing but instinct to rely on.
