About the Author

Hello from Mountainwood.

Sarah Lundin writes the Mountainwood Mysteries, a cozy mystery series set in a small New England inn town where the coffee is always hot and the murder rate is, admittedly, a little high. She's the author of eight books in the series so far, with more on the way.

When she's not plotting Elsie Harper's next case, she can usually be found somewhere warmer than New England, testing recipes, and being generally unimpressed by anyone who claims their dog is smarter than Murphy.

Behind the Series

Why Mountainwood?

Every cozy mystery needs a place worth returning to. Mountainwood was built as exactly that — a small New England town with a working inn, a cast of neighbors who all have something to hide, and a border collie who never lets a mystery go unsolved for long. Each book stands on its own, but the town and its people keep growing with every visit.
